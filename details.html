Analytics at a Glance

Zenkoro offers powerful features to simplify self-custody portfolio management. From unified multi-chain tracking to detailed profit analysis, everything you need is available in one secure application. Here are some of the highlights:

  • Multi-Chain Tracking: Consolidate all your holdings across multiple blockchains in one dashboard. Track assets on Ethereum, Solana, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, Sui, and more – no more juggling apps or missing out on parts of your portfolio.
  • Asset Allocation Breakdowns: Visualize your asset distribution at a glance. See exactly what percentage of your wealth is in Bitcoin, Ether, altcoins, or stablecoins. Get clear breakdowns by asset, category, or network to inform smarter diversification decisions.
  • Transaction Fee Summaries: Keep tabs on gas and transaction costs across all your activities. Zenkoro automatically aggregates what you spend on fees on each network, so you know the true cost of your trades and transfers over time.
  • Smart Portfolio Insights: Leverage intelligent analytics to spot trends and opportunities in your portfolio. Identify your top performers and underperforming assets instantly. Zenkoro’s smart insights highlight important changes to help you make data-driven decisions.
  • Profit/Loss Analysis: Monitor your gains and losses with comprehensive P/L tracking. View your portfolio’s performance over time and track both realized and unrealized profits. Whether you’re up 50% or facing a dip, you’ll have the context behind the numbers to plan your next move confidently.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Zenkoro’s pricing model?

Zenkoro is currently free to use for all core features. We believe everyone should have access to advanced crypto tracking.

How does Zenkoro keep my data and assets secure?

Security is our top priority. Zenkoro is a non-custodial solution – we never ask for your private keys, and we cannot move or control your assets. When you connect a wallet, we only request read-only access to your public wallet data. In short, your keys and funds stay entirely under your control while using Zenkoro.

Which blockchains and DeFi platforms does Zenkoro support?

Zenkoro works across a broad range of blockchain networks. You can track assets on major chains like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, Solana, Sui, and many others. We continuously add support for new networks and Layer-2 solutions as they emerge. In addition, Zenkoro recognizes assets in popular DeFi protocols on those networks – from DEX liquidity pools to staking contracts – giving you a comprehensive view of all your crypto holdings in one place.

How do I get started with Zenkoro?

Getting started with Zenkoro is simple and quick. There’s no lengthy sign-up or personal data required – just connect your crypto wallet (you can connect multiple wallets) using our secure web or mobile app. The platform will automatically aggregate your balances, transaction history, and DeFi positions into your dashboard within seconds. Prefer not to connect directly? You can alternatively input a public wallet address to monitor that portfolio. Either way, you’ll be up and running with your personal crypto dashboard almost instantly. Zenkoro won’t ever ask for private keys or permissions to move funds.
